Core Features

Built for precision
designed for speed

Everything you need to go from raw numbers to stunning visual graphs in seconds.

📈
2D GRAPH PLOTTING
Gradient spectra line charts with glowing data points, area fill, auto-scaled axes and coordinate tooltips on hover or touch.
🧊
3D SCATTER PLOT
Fully interactive Three.js 3D visualization. Drag to rotate, pinch/scroll to zoom, color-coded XYZ axes with auto-rotation.
📴
100% OFFLINE
Three.js is bundled locally. No CDN, no internet required. Your graph data stays on your device, always.
💾
PERSISTENT STATE
Points auto-saved between sessions via localStorage. Reopen the app and your last graph is right there waiting.
🎨
GHOST SPECTRA THEME
Signature cyan→violet→amber spectral palette with starfield background and ghost branding. Dark. Dramatic. Yours.
⌨️
KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS
Full keyboard control on Windows — Ctrl+P to plot, Ctrl+N to add, Ctrl+2/3 to switch modes. Power-user ready.

Workflow

From numbers
to spectra in seconds

01
CHOOSE YOUR DIMENSION
Switch between 2D (X, Y) and 3D (X, Y, Z) mode with a single tap or keyboard shortcut. The input fields adapt instantly.
02
ADD YOUR DATA POINTS
Tap ADD POINT and enter your coordinates. Add as many as you need — points are auto-saved as you type.
03
PLOT THE SPECTRA
Hit PLOT and watch your data transform into a glowing spectral graph. 2D renders a gradient line chart, 3D renders an interactive scatter plot.
04
EXPLORE & INTERACT
Hover or touch data points to inspect exact values. In 3D: drag to rotate, pinch/scroll to zoom — auto-rotates when idle.

The Developer

Developed by
Amlan Dey

GhostSpectraX started as a simple mobile graph tool and grew into a cross-platform visualizer with a signature ghost identity. Built with a passion for clean code, offline-first design, and memorable aesthetics.

Every pixel of the ghost theme — the cyan glow, the spectral gradient, the starfield — was crafted to make data visualization feel alive.
